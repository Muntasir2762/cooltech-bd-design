Company Overview

Cooltech Corporation is a leading Air Conditioning Company in Bangladesh Since 2005. From the very beginning, we have started our journey with the Residential Type Air Conditioner. Within a couple of years, we have expanded our business and started to serve light commercial to Industrial Air Conditioning solution to our valuable customers.

Cooltech has covered all aspect of work in the area of Mechanical installations including HVAC systems. During this time Cooltech has become specialists in planned preventative installation, testing, commissioning and maintenance.

Our success is based on the continuous commitment that services give to all our clients by continually updating and developing our rising cost-competitive pricing structure.

Services

We import the best quality air conditioners from overseas and sell it to the local market. So our services are involved with importing, indenting, supply, and maintenance which includes fixing air conditioners, VRF/ VRV system, ventilation & exhaust, HVAC duct cleaning, clean-rooms, hospitals & controlled areas, HVAC chilled & hot water system, HVAC system leak testing, clean & adjust burner/ pilot assembly, clean ignition assembly, perform safety inspection of heat exchanger, monitor flue draft, adjust operating pressures, monitor starting capabilities, test safety controls, test for proper airflow, clean or replace air filters specific to contract, clean/ adjust blower component, monitor voltage/ amperage, clean evaporator coils once a year, clean condenser coils once a year, measure temperature rise, and inspection & replacement of drive belts.
